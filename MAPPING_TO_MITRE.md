# Mapping to MITRE ATT&CK

This framework maps to the following MITRE ATT&CK tactics:

- Reconnaissance
- Initial Access
- Execution
- Persistence
- Privilege Escalation
- Defense Evasion
- Credential Access
- Discovery
- Lateral Movement
- Collection
- Command and Control
- Exfiltration
- Impact

The framework places particular emphasis on **Defense Evasion** and **Command and Control** due to their importance in long-term, stealthy operations.

This mapping helps teams align their activities with common detection strategies and improve purple team collaboration.
